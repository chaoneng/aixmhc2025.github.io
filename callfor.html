Call for Industrial Session Proposals

Second International Conference on AI x Medicine, Health, and Care


Call for Industrial Sessions

As the World Health Organization (WHO) states: "Health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity." With the rapid advances in AI technologies, including innovations like ChatGPT, now is the opportune moment to explore the broader impacts of AI on various aspects of health and well-being. This conference aims to bridge the gap between AI and its applications in medicine, mental health, and social well-being, highlighting the interplay between these domains and fostering interdisciplinary collaboration.

2025 AIxMHC solicits proposals for half-day industrial sessions related to topics relevant to the Artificial Intelligence x Medicine, Health, and Care (AIxMHC), is an international forum for academia and industries to exchange visions and ideas in the state of the art and practice, as well as to identify the emerging topics and define the future of how AI can assist medicine, health and care. Interested organizers are invited to submit their industrial session proposals in the topic areas listed in the Call for Papers of the conference(https://aixmhc2025.asia.edu.tw/submission.html).

The proposal for an industrial session should include title, abstract, organizers and a list of potential contributors to the session. The industrial session proposal must be submitted electronically by following the instructions available in the conference website (https://aixmhc2025.asia.edu.tw/submission.html).The deadline is May 30, 2025.


Submission Guidelines

Proposals for Industrial Sessions should include:
1. Session Title
2. Session Organizer(s): Name, affiliation, and contact information.
3. Session Abstract: A brief description of the session, its objectives, and its relevance to the conference theme (max. 500 words).
4. List of Proposed Speakers: Including names, affiliations, and topics of their presentations.
5. Session Format: Specify the format of the session (e.g., panel discussion, presentations, workshop) and any special requirements.


Join Us

We encourage industry leaders, innovators, and researchers to submit their proposals and share their insights on the evolving role of AI in health and care. Together, let's define the future of AI in enhancing the quality of life across the globe.
